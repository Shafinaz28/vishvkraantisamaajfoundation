Our Story

Founded in 2020 (formally registered in 2026) by Bondili Sai Manoj and Prasad Majji, Vishv Kraanti Samaaj Foundation began with one dream: rural children should never be left behind due to lack of resources.

From our beginnings in Andhra Pradesh, we have grown into a movement that guides rural students toward urban education, real-world skills, and financial independence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our work, donations, and how you can get involved.

Who does Vishv Kraanti Samaaj Foundation support?
We support rural children and students—especially from Andhra Pradesh—who need help accessing urban education, skill training, mentorship, and financial guidance to build independent futures.
What programs do you run?
Our main initiatives include the Rural-to-Urban Education Bridge, Skill Development Workshops, and Financial Literacy & Stability programs. Visit our Programs page to learn more.
How can I donate?
You can donate online through our Donate page or transfer directly to our foundation bank account (AU Small Finance Bank). Every contribution supports scholarships, workshops, and student support.
Can I volunteer or mentor students?
Yes. Volunteers help with mentoring, workshops, and program coordination. Reach out via our Contact page or email info@vksfoundation.org.
Where is the foundation based?
We work across India with roots in Andhra Pradesh (Rajahmundry) and a presence in Bengaluru, Karnataka. See full addresses on our Contact page.
How can I partner with VKS Foundation (CSR / schools)?
Companies, schools, and community groups can partner with us for CSR initiatives, sponsorships, and awareness drives. Call +91 93530 85494 or write to us through the contact form.
