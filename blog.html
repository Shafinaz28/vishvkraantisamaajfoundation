Education • Mar 2026

Bridging the Gap: Rural Students in Urban Schools

Moving from a village school to an urban college can feel overwhelming. Through scholarships, travel support, and mentor check-ins, VKS Foundation helps students navigate fees, language barriers, and the confidence gap that many rural youth face in city classrooms.

Finance • Feb 2026

Why Financial Literacy Matters for Rural Youth

Our financial independence workshops teach students how to budget stipends, save for emergencies, and plan for higher education. These habits ripple outward—many students share what they learn with parents and siblings at home.

Skills • Jan 2026

Skill Workshops That Open Career Doors

From basic computer skills to interview preparation, our workshops are designed for students who have talent but limited exposure. Volunteers and professionals guide sessions that build real-world readiness.

Mentorship • Dec 2025

How Mentors Shape Student Confidence

A consistent mentor can change how a student sees their future. Our volunteer mentors check in on academics, personal challenges, and career goals—offering guidance that families may not always be able to provide alone.

Stories • Nov 2025

Scholarship Support: Changing One Family at a Time

When school fees are covered, entire households breathe easier. Sponsored students often become the first in their family to pursue higher education—and inspire younger siblings to follow.

Foundation • Oct 2025

VKS Foundation's Journey Since 2020

What began as a dream in Andhra Pradesh has grown into a registered Section 8 NGO supporting 100+ students. Our founders believed rural talent deserves urban opportunity—and that belief still drives every program today.
